Bidar is a city in the north-eastern part of Karnataka state in India. It is the headquarters of Bidar district which borders Maharashtra and Telangana. It is a rapidly urbanising city in the wider Bidar Metropolitan area. The city is well known for its many sites of architectural, historical and religious importance.
